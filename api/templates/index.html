Introduction

Ensuring fish freshness is essential for quality and safety in the seafood industry, and the condition of a fish's eyes is a crucial indicator of freshness. Fresh fish have clear, bulging eyes, while deteriorating fish show signs such as cloudiness, sunken eyes, or a milky appearance. As fish age, their eyes become cloudy due to the breakdown of tissues and biochemical changes. Advances in technology now enable the use of computer vision and artificial intelligence to assess freshness by analyzing these visual cues. This approach enhances quality control and helps ensure that only the freshest fish reach consumers, thereby improving safety and reducing waste.
